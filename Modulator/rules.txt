Rules for Interacting with others

1. Do not organize, participate in, or encourage harassment of others.

2. Do not organize, promote, or coordinate servers around hate speech.

3. Do not make threats of violence or threaten to harm others

4. Do not evade user blocks or server bans.

5. Do not send others viruses or malware

Rules for content on Discord

6. You must apply the NSFW label to channels if there is adult content in that channel.

7. You may not sexualize minors in any way.

8. You may not share sexually explicit content of other people without their consent

9. You may not share content that glorifies or promotes suicide or self-harm

10. You may not share images of sadistic gore or animal cruelty.

11. You may not use Discord for the organization, promotion, or support of violent extremism.

12. You may not operate a server that sells or facilitates the sales of prohibited or potentially dangerous goods.

13. You may not promote, distribute, or provide access to content involving the hacking, cracking, or distribution of pirated software or stolen accounts.

14.  you should not promote, encourage or engage in any illegal behavior.

15. You may not sell your account or your server.

16. You may not use self-bots or user-bots to access Discord.

17. You may not share content that violates anyone's intellectual property or other rights.

18. You may not spam Discord
